Real predictions from real data. A gamechanger in the industry.

The New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM) published data from an ACRIN Cancer Research Group trial for identifying positive and negative polyp cases, which was later made available to a wider research community to allow for a larger audience to work on generating insights from the data.

Colon cancer is the third leading cause of cancer-related deaths in the United States. When detected early, colorectal cancer is highly preventable.

Up to 85% of colorectal cancers can be prevented or successfully treated if all eligible individuals got screened early and often.
